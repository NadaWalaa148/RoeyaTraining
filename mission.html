Our Mission

To improve the healthcare system for the well being of our society through establishing state of the art training centers and providing training, knowledge for our young physicians to further improve the quality of patient care .

Vision

Roeya Training Center is specialized training center in the field of Endoscopy aiming to enhance the capabilities of endoscopists to meet international standards.

ROEYA Training center is the first center in Egypt specialized in Hands on Training.
